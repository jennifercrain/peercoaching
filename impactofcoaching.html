What is Peer Coaching?

Coaching focuses on empowering students to develop effective learning strategies and self-management skills. Peer coaches work with students to identify their learning challenges, set achievable goals, and overcome obstacles hindering academic success.

The emphasis is on building metacognitive skills, such as time management, organization, and study techniques, enabling students to become independent and self-directed learners.

Why Peer Coaching?

Peer-to-peer support offers a powerful and often underutilized resource in academic settings.

Academic Performance

Studies have demonstrated that peer tutoring significantly improves academic performance, with research indicating increased knowledge retention and deeper comprehension.

Topping, 2005

Zone of Proximal Development

Vygotsky's ZPD highlights the importance of learning within a supportive social context, suggesting students achieve greater understanding when working with more knowledgeable peers.

Vygotsky, 1978

Self-Efficacy & Motivation

Students who participate in peer-to-peer learning often have higher levels of self-efficacy and motivation as they witness peers succeeding and realize their own potential.

Bandura, 1997

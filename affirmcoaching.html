What is AFFIRM?

AFFIRM is WGU's very own coaching framework for academic success. A framework helps guide your coaching sessions by providing a clear structure and sequence. Instead of wondering what to do next, the framework gives you a roadmap that keeps both you and the student focused, intentional, and moving toward their learning goals. It helps ensure consistency in your approach and creates a supportive environment where students feel guided and understood.

The AFFIRM framework has six interconnected stages, each building on the last. Think of it as a fluid process—you may find yourself moving back and forth between stages as you clarify goals, re-explain strategies, or deepen understanding. The framework adapts to the student's needs.

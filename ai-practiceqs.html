Student says:

"My Course Instructor told me to make questions using AI."

Response Guidelines

Response Approach

  • • Encourage the student to follow their Course Instructor's guidance and seek clarification directly from them if needed.
  • • Do not advise on how to use AI to generate questions.
  • • Redirect the session to skill development.

Example redirect: "Let's focus on how you'll approach and analyze those questions once you have them."

Support the Student By

  • • Reviewing test-taking strategies from the Academic Coaching Center
  • • Practice applying those strategies to practice questions within the course materials
  • • Show the student how to locate information within their course to confirm their understanding

Key Focus: The focus remains on how the student thinks and engages with WGU's materials, not how AI generates content.

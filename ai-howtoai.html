Student says:

"Do you know how I can use AI at WGU?"

Response Guidelines

Response Approach

  • • Do not simply "tell" the student WGU policies.
  • • Redirect to resources in the Academic Coaching Center, ACC Knowledge Base, and Student Handbook.

Support the Student By

  • • Modeling how to access the Student Handbook
  • • Demonstrating how to search the ACC & Knowledge Base
  • • Encouraging them to ask their Course Instructor or Program Mentor for further guidance

Key Focus: Empower students to find answers independently by directing them to resources rather than providing the information directly.

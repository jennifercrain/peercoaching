Peer Coaching Structure

You will have an entire support system during your time as a peer coach! Each intern has an assigned Manager, Supervisor, and Peer Coach IIs. You will see these roles mentioned throughout your training.

Organization Chart

Senior Manager

Our senior manager is Lynn Kelly, who focuses on long-term goals, strategy, and big decisions that move our entire department forward. She guides the managers with team priorities, policies, and projects to make sure we are aligned with the vision of the University and fulfilling the commitments to our department. She supports the Director of Academic Services.

Academic Services includes Peer Coaching, Academic Coaching, Orientation, Student Communities, and Social Emotional Learning.

Managers

This is your day-to-day leader. They hired you! We have managers in different areas, making sure everyone in their downline knows what to do and when. They guide the Supervisors and support their team of interns with regular check-ins to discuss progress, expectations, career connections, and problem-solving. The Managers support the Senior Manager with the implementation, monitoring, and analysis of team priorities, policies, projects, and quality performance.

Supervisors

The Supervisors are full-time professionals with expertise in coaching, training, and/or product and process development. They implement program priorities, guide the work of the Peer Coach II, and support their team of interns with performing their best. They play a key role in operational execution and have the authority to make decisions within the area they support to ensure program effectiveness. Supervisors support the Managers with the implementation of strategic priorities and day-to-day decision-making.

Peer Coach II

This is a high-achieving intern who demonstrates organizational skills, the capacity for multifaceted support, and who exhibits a high degree of Integrity, Courage, and Urgency, three WGU Leadership Principles critical to this role. The Peer Coach II takes on some extra responsibilities to assist the work of the Supervisors while still providing student support through Peer Coaching appointments or live events.

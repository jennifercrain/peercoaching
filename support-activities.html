Week Three Training

Support Activities

Beyond live events and appointments—additional ways you'll support students and grow your coaching skills

Your Support Role

In addition to supporting students during live events or appointments, you will also spend time participating in a variety of support activities.

📌 Important: Some activities and expectations may vary by team, and your leadership team will provide guidance specific to your role.

Support Activities

Outreach Calling

Some teams include Outreach Calling as part of daily support activities. As covered in the Outreach Calling section of training, outreach may focus on specific groups of students, such as:

  • Students who are new to their course
  • Students who have had unsuccessful OA or PA attempts

Student Touchpoints

Peer Coaches may also spend time connecting — or reconnecting — with students outside of scheduled appointments or live events. This may include:

  • Following up with students from previous appointments and encouraging them to share progress updates or schedule another session
  • Reaching out to students who cancelled appointments to encourage them to reconnect
  • Contacting students who enrolled in live events but did not attend and inviting them to join a future session
  • Checking in with students who attended a live event to encourage continued progress and future participation

Training

Throughout your internship, you may be assigned additional training opportunities based on program updates or areas of growth. These trainings are designed to help you continue building your coaching skills and confidence.

As Assigned by Leadership

Your leadership team may also assign additional tasks or projects as needed. This could include activities such as:

  • Assisting with projects
  • Creating sizzle reels
  • Reflecting on calls or live events
  • Completing other team support tasks

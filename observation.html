Peer Coaching Observations

Shadow appointments and live events to develop your peer coaching skills

Next, you will sign up to shadow TWO appointments or live events. Plan ahead! Each peer coach on your team was instructed to share their calendar with you when you began training and live event schedules are available on SharePoint. If you cannot see them, please reach out to your Supervisor.

If shadowing appointments, reach out to the Peer Coach on Teams. They will invite you to a Teams meeting where they share their audio and screen during their appointment. If shadowing live events, add your name to the observation column on your course schedule. You will join the live event (listed in Mountain Time) using the course's Webex button on SharePoint.

Observation Setup

Appointment Observations

Sign up to shadow two appointments from team's peer coaches. Ask them directly via Teams message to observe their call.

Setup Steps:

  1. 1. Find appointments on team calendars
  2. 2. Message the peer coach to ask permission
  3. 3. Click appointment to see course & focus area
  4. 4. Listen intently and complete observation form
  5. 5. Consider what you would do differently

Calendar Naming:

[Your Name] 1:1 Observation [Coach Name]

Example: Jarrad Markel 1:1 Observation Shelby Hulland

Toggle "Teams meeting" ON for 1:1 observations

Note: Camera and microphone not required. Reflections are confidential between you and your Supervisor.

Live Event Observations

Sign up to shadow two 60-minute live events over the next week. Most courses run sessions Sunday through Wednesday. Sign up early to maximize your options.

Setup Steps:

  1. 1. Review Discussion Map Template
  2. 2. Check Peer Coaching Team SharePoint for schedules
  3. 3. Add initials to PC Shadow column
  4. 4. Copy Webex link to calendar event
  5. 5. Toggle "Teams meeting" OFF

Calendar Naming:

[Course Code] [Session Title] Observation ([Coach Name])

Example: D082 Making the Most of Your Time Observation (Shelby)

Note: Camera and microphone not required. Your honest reflections are strictly between you and your Supervisor.

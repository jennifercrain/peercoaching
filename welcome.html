Welcome!

Welcome to this exciting journey of becoming a peer coach at WGU! We're thrilled to have you join us. Over the next few weeks, you'll gain the knowledge and skills to empower others, foster growth, and make a positive impact.

Through this program, you will develop the skills to cultivate meaningful peer relationships, utilize powerful coaching strategies, and play a vital role in your peers' academic achievements. We can't wait to see you flourish as a skilled and compassionate peer coach!

This hands-on training encourages active participation. Stay focused, limit distractions, and reflect on the material to maximize your learning. By holding yourself accountable, you'll not only support your peers but also enhance your own understanding. Please be fully present during training.

Training Expectations

Sequential Training

You must complete all training in the provided order. Do not skip ahead. Remember to complete and submit all assignments and click the 'Mark as Done' button at the bottom of each page (when it's provided) before proceeding to the next to ensure your progress is recorded.

Upon completion of the week's training, do not move forward to the next week. This sequential design allows for a focused understanding of specific aspects of your role. Please contact your Supervisor if you complete training early.

Quizzes and Progress

Please note: You will encounter quizzes throughout the course. Each quiz has limited attempts and, sometimes, a cooldown period before it can be taken again. Your progress and responses will be reviewed by your leaders each week.

Peer Coaching Models

WGU offers two types of Peer Coaching, each with its own focus and approach.

Live Events

Peer Coaching live events are centered around learning strategies and helping students develop better study skills. Peer Coaches use various strategies that encourage students to engage with the course material and their fellow peers.

Appointments

Peer Coaching appointments center around helping students to problem-solve, set goals, and improve their overall performance and success at WGU in a one-on-one setting. Peer Coaches use various strategies that encourage students to think critically and self-select solutions that work best for them.
